# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

CITADEL is for everyone. We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for all contributors, regardless of experience level, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or nationality.

## Our Standards

**Do:**
- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome newcomers — especially vibe coders and non-traditional developers
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community
- Show empathy

**Don't:**
- Harass, troll, or insult others
- Publish others' private information
- Use sexualized language or imagery
- Engage in political attacks or derogatory comments

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code. Repeated or serious violations may result in a ban.

Report issues to the maintainers via GitHub Issues marked `[CoC]`.
